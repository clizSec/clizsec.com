CryptoZoom is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our app.

CryptoZoom is designed to provide users with the ability to track cryptocurrency prices of specific coins selected by the user or app user. No personal information is collected or exchanged besides the user's requests to the API for cryptocurrency price data.
